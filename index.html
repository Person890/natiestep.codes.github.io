About

I am a student. For the majority of the day I sit in front of my computer researching, exploring, experimenting, sketching and coding. Every day I try my best to combine the aesthetics of art with the beautiful logic of algorithms in my projects. Originally, my career was centered in a medical field. However, it didn't take long for me to realize that the look of blood and needles terrify me. Thankfully, I had the opportunity to travel to the US and enjoy a whole year studying and working in the Bay Area. The change of environment was not only refreshing, but it was a crucial point in my life in which I realized what my passion is. As of summer 2017, I was enrolled in my first programming class - Introduction to Java Programming. Needless to say, I fell off-track after couple of lectures and shortly started to feel desperately lost. It would be impossible for me to continue in this new path without help and support of people around me.
